Provolone & Mushroom Chicken

Provolone & Mushroom Chicken

2 teaspoons olive oil

½ teaspoon salt

¼ teaspoon black pepper and ¼ teaspoon ground red pepper

4 6-oz skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

8 slices of toasted sourdough bread

4 slices provolone cheese

¼ cup trimmed arugula

Mushroom sauce

2 8-oz pkgs mushrooms

1 tablespoon olive oil

¼ cup finely chopped shallots

1 ½ teaspoons chopped fresh thyme

2 garlic cloves, minced

½ teaspoon salt

¼ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

½ cup light sour cream

Place mushrooms in a food processor and process until finely chopped. Heat olive oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Add mushrooms, shallots, thyme, and garlic; cook for 10 minutes or until liquid evaporates. Stirring occasionally. Stir in salt and pepper.

Place mushroom mixture in a bowl, stir in sour cream.

(also great as a dip, serve at room temperature or chilled)

Heat 2 teaspoons olive oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Rub salt, and peppers evenly over chicken. Place chicken in pan, cook 4 minutes on each side or until done. Spread mushroom sauce over each toasted bread slice, top with a chicken breast half, a slice of cheese and arugula and top with another slice of bread.
