Apple Cider-Brined Turkey

Apple Cider-Brined Turkey

BRINE

12 cups apple cider

1 1/3 cup kosher salt

1 1/3 cup sugar

1 1/2 tablespoon black peppercorns, coarsely crushed

1 1/2 tablespoon whole allspice, coarsely crushed

12 (1/8-inch-thick) slices peeled fresh ginger

9 whole cloves

3 bay leaves

1 (20-pound) fresh or frozen turkey, thawed

3 oranges, quartered

9 cups ice

2 Turkey-sized oven bags

Remaining ingredients:
6 garlic cloves
6 sage leaves
6 thyme sprigs
6 parsley sprigs
1 onion, quartered
1 1/2 (14-ounce) can fat-free, less-sodium chicken broth
3 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted and divided
1 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper, divided
1 teaspoon salt, divided

MAKE BRINE THE NIGHT BEFORE putting in with the turkey...it takes a long time to cool!

Combine first 8 ingredients (through bay leaves) in a large saucepan; bring to a boil. Cook 5 minutes or until sugar and salt dissolve. Cool completely.

Remove giblets and neck from turkey; reserve for gravy if desired. Rinse turkey with cold water; pat dry. Trim excess fat. Stuff body cavity with orange quarters. Place a turkey-sized oven bag inside a second bag to form a double thickness. Place bags in a large stockpot. Place turkey inside inner bag. Add cider mixture and ice. Secure bags with several twist ties. Refrigerate for 24 hours, turning occasionally.

Preheat oven to 500o.

Remove turkey from bags, and discard brine, orange quarters, and bags. Rinse turkey with cold water; pat dry. Lift wing tips up and over back; tuck under turkey. Tie legs together with kitchen string. Place garlic, sage, thyme, parsley, onion, and broth in the bottom of a roasting pan. Place roasting rack in pan. Arrange turkey, breast side down, on a roasting rack. Brush turkey back with 1 tablespoon butter; sprinkle with ½ teaspoon pepper and ¼ teaspoon salt. Bake at 500o for 30 minutes.

Reduce oven temperature to 350 o.

Remove turkey from oven. Carefully turn turkey over (breast side up) using tongs. Brush turkey breast with 1 tablespoon butter; sprinkle with ½ teaspoon pepper and ¼ teaspoon salt. Bake at 350o for 1 hour and 15 minutes or until a thermometer inserted into meaty part of thigh registers 170o (make sure not to touch bone). (Shield the turkey with foil if it browns too quickly.) Remove turkey from oven; let stand 20 minutes. Reserve pan drippings for gravy if 4r desired. Discard skin before serving.
