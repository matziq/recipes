California Roll Sushi Bowl

California Roll Sushi Bowl

Yield: 2

Ingredients

  • 1/4 Cup Soy Sauce
  • 2 Tablespoons Sugar
  • 1 Tablespoon Rice Vinegar
  • 2 Cups Cooked White Rice* - cooled
  • 1/2 Cup Chopped Imitation Crab
  • 1/2 Cucumber - peeled (seeded and chopped)
  • 1/4 Small Lemon - sliced extra thin then finely chopped (peel included)
  • 1 Tablespoon Pickled Sushi Ginger - chopped
  • 1/2 Avocado - sliced or cut into chunks
  • 1 Sheet Dried Seaweed - crumbled
  • Toasted Sesame Seeds - to taste

Instructions

  1. [Sauce]
  2. Combine sauce ingredients in a small pot over medium heat. While continuously whisking, bring to a boil and reduce to a slightly syrupy consistency. This will take approximately 3 minutes. Do not walk away as this can easily burn. Remove from heat and allow to cool while assembling sushi bowls.
  3. [Sushi Bowls]
  4. Combine all ingredients and top with a drizzle of soy sauce mixture.
