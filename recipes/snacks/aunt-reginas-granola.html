Aunt Regina's Granola

Aunt Regina’s Granola

3/4 Cup Brown Sugar (packed)

2 Cups Sliced Almonds

2 Cups Broken Cashews

2 Cups Ribbon Coconut

1 1/2 tsp. REAL Sea Salt

6 Cups Old Fashioned Rolled Oats

Mix well in large bowl, then add:

1/2 Cup Olive or Canola Oil

3/4 Cup Maple Syrup--Mixed together.

Bake at 250 for 1 hour and 15 minutes.

Stir every 15 minutes.

Divide evenly onto 2 cookie sheets to bake.

Watch closely, this can burn very easily.
