Cookie Butter Frosting

Cookie Butter Frosting

Incredibly sweet and delicious buttercream frosting flavored with Biscoff cookie butter. You’ll want to put this frosting on EVERYTHING!

Yield: 24 • Prep: 15 min • Total: 15 min

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter (softened)
  • 1 cup Biscoff Cookie Butter
  • 2 cup confectioners’ sugar
  • 4 tablespoons heavy cream (room temperature)

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, beat the butter with an electric mixer on medium speed until creamy and smooth, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the cookie butter and beat on medium speed until well combined, about 3 minutes.
  3. Gradually beat in the powdered sugar. The mixture will become somewhat thick. Continue beating on medium speed.
  4. Beat in one tablespoon of the cream at a time, scraping down the sides of the bowl as needed, until a fluffy and smooth consistency is achieved.
