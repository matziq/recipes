Mexican Black Bean Dip

Mexican Black Bean Dip

1 15.5 oz. can black beans

1/3 C. medium-hot salsa

¼ C. cilantro leaves

4 tsp. lemon juice

1 small garlic l clove, minced

Cayenne pepper to taste

Drain and rinse the black beans. Combine the black beans, salsa, cilantro leave, lemon juice, and garlic in a food processor. Blend until smooth. Makes 1 ¼ cups and serves 4-6. Use with chips or vegetables.
