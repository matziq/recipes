Sweet & Spicy Sriracha Chili Dipping Sauce Recipe

Sweet & Spicy Sriracha Chili Dipping Sauce Recipe

This sweet and spicy sriracha chili dipping sauce is so addictive! Any plain food comes alive when you dip it into this amazing homemade sweet and spicy sauce! This sweet chili sauce takes just 5 minutes to make and tastes amazing!

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup brown sugar
  • 3 tbsp bottled hot sauce
  • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder

Instructions

  1. Put all ingredients in a saucepan. Bring to a boil over medium heat, then stir with a spoon for 1 minute or until the sugar is fully dissolved. Remove from heat and serve.
