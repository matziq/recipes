Authentic Homemade Ranchero Sauce Recipe

Authentic Homemade Ranchero Sauce Recipe

This is the best ranchero sauce recipe I have ever tasted, with a base of diced tomato, jalapeno and onion, seasoned with a mixture of chili powders and seasonings. Perfect on huevos rancheros or any Mexican or Tex Mex dish.

Yield: 14 • Prep: 5 min • Cook: 25 min • Total: 30 min

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium yellow onion (chopped)
  • 2 jalapeno peppers (chopped)
  • 4 cloves garlic (chopped)
  • 16 ounce can diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • ¼ cup chopped cilantro (or more as desired)
  • 1 tablespoon ancho powder
  • 1 tablespoon guajillo powder
  • 1 tablespoon chipotle powder
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne
  • 1 teaspoon Mexican oregano
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Juice from 1 lime (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat a large pan to medium-high heat and add the olive oil.
  2. Cook the onions and peppers down about 5 minutes to soften.
  3. Add the garlic and cook another minute, stirring a bit.
  4. Add the tomato and chicken broth. Stir.
  5. Stir in the cilantro and seasonings.
  6. Bring to a quick boil then reduce the heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes to let the flavors develop. The sauce will thicken up for you.
  7. Transfer the mixture to a blender or food processor and pour in the lime juice. I used my stick blender.
  8. Process until nice and smooth.
  9. Use immediately or store in the refrigerator in a covered glass container.
