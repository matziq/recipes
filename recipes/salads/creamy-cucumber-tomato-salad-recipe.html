Creamy Cucumber Tomato Salad Recipe

Creamy Cucumber Tomato Salad Recipe

Crunchy cucumber, juicy tomatoes and crisp red onions with lighter homemade Ranch dressing make this delicious and flavorful salad a favorite!More salad recipes: www.crunchycreamysweet.com

Yield: 4 people • Prep: 10 min • Total: 10 min

Ingredients

  • 1 cucumber
  • 2 large Roma tomatoes (see note)
  • 1/2 red onion
  • 1/2 teaspoon minced garlic (optional)
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley
  • 1/2 cup natural sour cream or Greek yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice (from 1/2 lemon)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried dill or 1 teaspoon fresh dill

Instructions

  1. Chop cucumber and tomatoes into bite size pieces. Place in a large salad bowl.
  2. Add chopped red onion and garlic, if using. Add parsley.
  3. In a measuring cup, whisk together sour cream, lemon juice, salt, pepper and dill. Pour over vegetables.
  4. Gently toss to coat.
  5. Serve salad immediately or cover bowl with plastic wrap and chill in fridge until ready to serve.
