Pesto Cavatappi

Pesto Cavatappi

Pesto Cavatappi - Copycat Noodles & Company pesto cavatappi that is ready in just minutes and great for a quick dinner or easy lunch!

Yield: 6 • Prep: 5 min • Cook: 20 min • Total: 25 min

Ingredients

  • 1 pound cavatappi pasta (or any short cut pasta)
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 8 oz mushrooms (thinly sliced)
  • 1 cup diced tomato
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup pesto
  • 1/2 cup shredded Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of water to boil and cook pasta according to package directions.
  2. In a skillet heat olive oil over medium high heat. Add tomatoes and mushrooms, cook for 5 minutes until the mushrooms are starting to brown.
  3. Add chicken broth and heavy cream. Bring to simmer and cook for 2-3 minutes until it starts to thicken.
  4. Remove from the heat and stir in pesto until well blended.
  5. Toss with drained pasta and top with Parmesan cheese to serve.
