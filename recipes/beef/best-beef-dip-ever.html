Best Beef Dip Ever

This beef dip recipe makes the best au jus in the slow cooker with a chuck roast, garlic, rosemary, bay leaf, and soy sauce; great for French dips.

Yield: 10 • Prep: 10 min • Cook: 360 min • Total: 370 min

Ingredients

  • 4 pounds beef chuck roast
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon dried rosemary
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 6 cups water
  • 1 cup soy sauce

Instructions

  1. Place beef in a slow cooker; season with garlic and rosemary.
  2. Add bay leaves; pour in water and soy sauce. Cook on Low for 6 to 10 hours.
