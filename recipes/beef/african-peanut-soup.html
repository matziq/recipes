African Peanut Soup

African Peanut Soup

2 onions

½-1 c oil

1-2 c water

1/2 t red pepper flakes

1/2 t garlic powder (or 4-5 cloves fresh garlic)

Salt & Pepper

1 lb. carrots

2 sweet potatoes

3 large russet potatoes

1 quart canned tomatoes

1/2 c peanut butter

Boneless cubed stew beef

Beef w/bones

Chop onions and brown in ½ cup hot oil. Keep stirring on high – when onions are very brown, add 1-2 cups of water. This will caramelize the onions. Simmer until onions are soft (usually 5-10 minutes).

Take onions out of the pot and keep them aside. In the pot, add more oil and beef. Brown the beef on high heat. When browned, cover with water nd add the onions back in. Add red pepper flakes, garlic, sale and pepper. Cook on medium heat until meat is tender.

After meat is tender, add veggies. First, carrots. 10 minutes later add sweet & russet potatoes and tomatoes.

Cook until the veggies are tender. Take out some of the liquid and add peanut butter to this liquid – stir together and incorporate back into the pot of soup.

Serve with couscous & bread…should be eaten with hands!
