Blueberry Brie Jalapeño Poppers

Blueberry Brie Jalapeño Poppers

A sweet and spicy appetizer featuring creamy brie, fresh blueberries, and jalapeños, baked until golden and bubbly.

Ingredients

  • 6 large jalapeños, halved lengthwise and seeded
  • 4 oz brie cheese, rind removed and cubed
  • 1/2 cup fresh blueberries
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1/4 cup chopped walnuts
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. [1. Prepare the Crust:]
  2. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  3. Arrange jalapeño halves on the baking sheet. Fill each half with brie cubes and top with blueberries.
  4. Drizzle with honey, sprinkle walnuts over the top, and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Bake for 15-20 minutes until brie is melted and jalapeños are tender. Serve warm.
