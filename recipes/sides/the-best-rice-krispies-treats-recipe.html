The BEST Rice Krispies Treats Recipe

The BEST rice krispie treats recipe. Classic rice krispietreats loaded with rice krispies cereal, marshmallows, and butter. Ready injust 10 minutes, this kid-approved dessert is so good.

Yield: 18 servings • Prep: 5 min • Cook: 10 min • Total: 15 min

Ingredients

  • 6 cups rice krispies cereal
  • 5 Tbsp unsalted butter
  • 10 oz mini marshmallows
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Line a 9×13 glass dish with parchment paper. Lightly grease and set aside.
  2. In a large pot, melt butter. On medium heat, add marshmallows to melted butter and stir until marshmallows are completely melted.
  3. Remove pot from heat and add the Rice Krispies cereal and vanilla. Mix quickly with a wooden spoon.
  4. Transfer the mixture to a prepared glass dish. Spread with a wooden spoon. Run your hands under cold water and with dampened hands press mixture down.
  5. Cut into squares and serve. Enjoy!
