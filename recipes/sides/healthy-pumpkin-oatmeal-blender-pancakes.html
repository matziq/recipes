Healthy Pumpkin Oatmeal Blender Pancakes

Healthy Pumpkin Oatmeal Blender Pancakes

Healthy Pumpkin Oatmeal Blender Pancakes

Yield: 6 pancakes • Prep: 0 hr 0 min • Cook: 0 hr 0 min

Ingredients

  • 1 c gluten free rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1/3 cup pumpkin puree
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp coconut oil, melted
  • 3/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp pumpkin pie spice
  • handful crushed walnuts, optional

Instructions

  1. Place all the ingredients (except walnuts) into a blender and blend on high until a smooth batter forms. Stir in walnuts if desired. Let the batter rest for 3-5 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, prepare a skillet or griddle with non-stick spray and turn on medium-low heat.
  3. Cook pancakes 1/4 cup at a time in preheated skillet. Cook until bubbles start to form, flip and cook the other side. Repeat until you have used all the batter. Serve immediately.
