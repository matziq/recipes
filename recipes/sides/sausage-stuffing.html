Sausage Stuffing

Sausage Stuffing

This sausage stuffing recipe is moist, flavorful, and easy to make with bread cubes, celery, onion, and savory sausage — perfect for every holiday.

Yield: 8 cups • Prep: 15 min • Cook: 15 min • Total: 30 min

Ingredients

  • 1 pound breakfast sausage
  • 0.75 cup melted margarine, or as needed
  • 1.5 cups finely diced celery
  • 0.75 cup chopped onion
  • 8 cups white bread cubes
  • 3 teaspoons poultry seasoning
  • 0.25 teaspoon ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook sausage until brown and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes. Transfer to a large bowl with a slotted spoon.
  2. Pour sausage drippings into a measuring cup; add enough butter to equal 1 cup. Pour into the skillet over medium heat.
  3. Sauté celery and onions in butter mixture until onion is tender; do not brown.
  4. Stir in 1/3 of the bread cubes.
  5. Transfer mixture to the bowl with sausage. Add remaining 2/3 bread cubes, poultry seasoning, and pepper; mix well.
