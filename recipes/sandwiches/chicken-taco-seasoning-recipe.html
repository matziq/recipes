Chicken Taco Seasoning Recipe

This easy Chicken Taco Seasoning Recipe is made with a perfect blend of pantry spices you likely already have on hand. Perfect for all your Mexican chicken dishes—whether it's ground chicken, breasts, or thighs, it adds bold, delicious flavor.

Yield: 4 Servings • Prep: 2 min • Total: 2 min

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt, use as per your taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Mexican oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper powder

Instructions

  1. Stir all ingredients together, use right away or store in air tight jar for later use.One recipe makes about 2 tablespoons or equivalent to 1 packet. It is also the perfect amount to season one pound of meat.
