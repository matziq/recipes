High‑Protein Buttermilk Pancakes (Your Mom’s Recipe, Upgraded)
(Makes the same amount, same flavor profile, but with a big protein boost)
Image: https://tishasveggieeats.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/08/img_4087-1.jpg

🥞 Adjusted Ingredients
- 1 whole egg  
- 1 egg white ← extra protein + fluff  
- 1 cup buttermilk  
- 2 tbsp avocado oil
- ⅔ cup flour  
- ⅓ cup whey protein powder (vanilla or unflavored works best)  
- 1 tbsp sugar  
- 1 tsp baking powder  
- ½ tsp baking soda  
- ½ tsp salt

Instructions (same as usual, with one tiny tweak)
1. Whisk egg + egg white.  
2. Add buttermilk + melted shortening.  
3. In another bowl, mix flour + protein powder + sugar + baking powder + baking soda + salt.  
4. Combine wet + dry gently (don’t overmix).  
5. Cook on medium heat until bubbles form; flip once.  

Optional Boosters (if you want even MORE protein)

- Add 2–3 tbsp Greek yogurt to the wet ingredients  
  → makes them extra tender + adds protein  

- Add 1–2 tbsp powdered milk  
  → invisible in flavor, adds protein

- Serve with peanut butter drizzle  
  → 1 tbsp PB = 4g protein 

- Add collagen (1 scoop)
